The Celestial Wayfinder

A DEF CON badge built for the world outside the grid. LoRa radio, GPS, compass, and a flashlight — in one hand-built device.

DEF CON Pickup

Find each other.
Off the grid.

The Celestial Wayfinder broadcasts your GPS coordinates over LoRa radio — no cell service, no internet, no infrastructure. Other Wayfinders in range receive your position and their LED compass ring swings to point directly at you.

  • LoRa mesh radio — self-healing, zero config, no infrastructure
  • GPS location broadcasting to every badge in range
  • LED ring compass points directly toward other badge holders
  • Sharp Memory-in-Pixel display — daylight readable, always on
  • SOS button — repeating distress ping every 15 seconds
  • Piezo buzzer alerts on incoming messages
  • 6-LED flashlight, 5× mechanical keyboard switches
  • ~1 hour charge time, smart auto-shutdown
